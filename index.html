What We'll Do

The Activities

☀️

Solar Walk

Journey through a scale model of the Solar System to explore the planets, learn about the vast distances of space, and discover our place in the universe. Scouts learn to set up and lead a Solar Walk — sharing this experience with younger Scouts, families, and the community as an act of service.

🔭

Stargazing

The evening concludes with hands-on stargazing using both traditional and smart telescopes. Participants will learn to locate, observe, and explain celestial objects, and prepare to host future astronomy events for others.

Advancement

Counts Toward Requirements

This outdoor event counts toward required outdoor events for advancement. It qualifies toward First and Second Class sign-offs (Requirement 1a), as well as selected requirements for the Astronomy Merit Badge.
